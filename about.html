Empowering the Youth

Naminjiwa Community Technical College is Phalombe’s leading institution for vocational excellence and industrial skills development.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible technical and vocational training that empowers the youth with practical skills for self-employment and industrial relevance in Malawi.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, producing innovative and skilled artisans who contribute significantly to the national economy.

Why Choose Naminjiwa TC?

Founded in Phalombe, Naminjiwa Community Technical College operates under the Ministry of Labour in close partnership with TEVETA Malawi. We focus on hands-on learning, ensuring that 80% of our curriculum is practical workshop experience.
